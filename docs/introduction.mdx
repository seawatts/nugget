## What is Nugget?

Nugget is a comprehensive parenting journey app that supports you through every phase - from trying to conceive, through pregnancy, to tracking your baby's feeding, sleep, and developmental milestones.

## Why Choose Nugget?

- **Privacy-First**: Your family's data is encrypted and never shared
- **Cross-Platform**: Web, iOS, and Android with seamless sync
- **Evidence-Based**: Built with input from healthcare professionals
- **Partner Sharing**: Collaborate with your partner on your parenting journey
- **Export Data**: Download your data anytime in multiple formats
